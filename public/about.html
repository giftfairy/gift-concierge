About Gift Lane

Gift Lane is your calm, luxe-feeling gifting concierge. It’s for people who don’t have time to scroll endless product pages, but still care deeply about giving something that feels personal and intentional.

How it works

You tell Gift Lane who the gift is for, what you’re celebrating, and your budget. Behind the scenes, an AI assistant curates ideas that are thoughtful, realistic, and aligned with Aussie brands, shipping, and budgets.

Why it exists

Gift Lane was created by a very tired mum who was sick of last-minute panic buys, generic hampers, and analysis paralysis. The goal is simple: make gifting feel easy again, without losing the heart behind it.
