Affiliate Disclosure

How affiliate links work on Gift Lane

We believe in being upfront about how this site earns money.

1. What are affiliate links?

Some products recommended on Gift Lane use affiliate links. This means if you click a link and go on to make a purchase, the retailer may pay Gift Lane a small commission for the referral.

This commission comes from the retailer, not from you. The price you pay is exactly the same as if you went to the site directly.

2. Why we use them

Affiliate income helps keep Gift Lane running and allows us to keep building better tools, guides, and ideas for time-poor humans who still want their gifts to feel considered.

3. How it affects our recommendations

Recommendations are driven by what feels thoughtful, realistic and appropriate for the person and budget you describe. We won’t suggest something purely because it has an affiliate program.

In some cases, we may link to multiple retailers so you can choose the store, shipping and pricing that works best for you.

4. Third-party tracking

When you click an affiliate link, a cookie may be placed in your browser by the retailer or affiliate network so they can recognise that Gift Lane referred you. We don’t see your payment details or account information.

5. Questions

If you have any questions about affiliate links on Gift Lane, you can email us at concierge@giftlane.au.
