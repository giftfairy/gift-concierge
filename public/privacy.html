Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Gift Lane (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

Gift Lane does not collect personal data unless you voluntarily submit it. When using the gift concierge, you may provide:

  • Details about the recipient (e.g., age, hobbies)
  • Occasion and budget information

None of this information identifies you personally. It is used only to generate curated gift suggestions.

2. How Your Information Is Used

Information submitted into the concierge form is sent securely to our AI assistant to generate suggestions. We do not store, sell, or share your personal details with any third party for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies & Analytics

Gift Lane may use anonymous analytics (e.g., page views, clicks) to improve the site experience. These do not identify you personally.

4. Affiliate Links

Some gift suggestions may contain affiliate links. If you choose to make a purchase, the retailer may track the sale to pay Gift Lane a commission. We do not receive any personal details about you from these partners.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information transmitted through the site. Gift Lane does not store sensitive personal information.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, email: concierge@giftlane.au.
